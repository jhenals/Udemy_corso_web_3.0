A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet in order of distance from the Sun and the largest of the terrestrial planets in the solar system , both by mass and by diameter . On its surface, there is water , present in all three states of aggregation ( solid , liquid and gaseous ) and an atmosphere mainly composed of nitrogen and oxygen which, together with the magnetic field that surrounds the planet, protects the Earth from cosmic rays and fromsolar radiation . As the only planetary body in the solar system capable of supporting life as conceived and known by humans , it is the only place where all known living species live.
